Saurabh Gupta

A renowned environmentalist and a social entrepreneur, Saurabh Gupta is the founder and director of Earth5R, and winner of the Young Leader India-France award, who is bringing a Paradigm Shift, one locality at a time.

Education Futurist

Dr. Coomi
Vevaina

Dr. Coomi Vevaina

An educator, storyteller, researcher, and author, Dr. Coomi Vevaina is the former head of the Department of English, MU. She has authored 10 books and 58 articles in various prestigious journals and has also authored several stories for children like “Bernice Banana” and “Angry Angara".

Visual Artist

Mehek
Malhotra

Mehak Malhotra

Mehek Malhotra is the CEO of Giggling Monkey Studio where she has designed many cool and popular logos that we see daily. She has won many accolades like the 25 under 25 by campus diaries, VH1 and TATA Nano in 2016.

Architect

Nuru
Karim

Nuru Karim

Nuru Karim is the founder and design principal of Nudes, a cross-disciplinary practice of public art, architecture-urbanism and design optimisation/fabrication powered by digital 'making' tools. His works have received several awards including a nomination for the Aga Khan Award for Architecture in the 2013 cycle.

Neurosurgeon

Mazda
Turel

Mazda Turel

Dr. Mazda K. Turel is a prolific researcher who has 80 publications in reputed scientific journals. His specialisation is the treatment of diseases of the brain and spine and is known for bringing a new approach to neurosurgery that is both balanced and aggressive.

Culture Editor at The Swaddle

Nadia
Nooreyezdan

Nadia Nooreyezdan

Nadia Nooreyezdan is the culture editor of The Swaddle, India’s only health and culture media platform with a strong feminist lens. Her articles cover issues at the intersection of gender and politics. She is invested in feminist theory, pop culture, and queering everything.

Kavish Seth is an independent artist, singer, songwriter, poet who writes primarily in Hindustani. Kavish invented his own instrument, Noori, a hybrid 14-stringed instrument to produce his own sound and connect with more people.
