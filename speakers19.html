Founder & Director - Earth5R

Saurabh
Gupta

Saurabh Gupta

A renowned environmentalist and a social entrepreneur, this young revolutionary left his lucrative job at Cafe Coffee Day to bicycle around different countries for 50,000 kilometres in search for a purpose. Meet Saurabh Gupta, the founder and director of Earth5R, a Mumbai based global environmental organization. Winner of the Young Leader India-France award by the President of France, Emmanuel Macron, and the project leader of Paris Peace Forum, this exciting man is bringing a Paradigm Shift, one locality at a time.

Education Futurist

Dr. Coomi
Vevaina

Dr. Coomi Vevaina

An educator, storyteller, researcher and author we are glad to present Dr. Coomi Vevaina as our second speaker. Dr. Vevaina is the former head of the Department of English, University of Mumbai. She has won several awards such as the G.S Rao memorial award for outstanding contribution to teaching of English (2012), Best Researcher and teacher in the University of Mumbai (2009), Award of merit from the Indian Association of Canadian studies to name a few. She has created “Wordfully Yours”, an association which aims to train adults in the art of storytelling and has also authored several stories for children like “Bernice Banana” and “Angry Angara”. She has authored 10 books and 58 articles in various prestigious journals. She is also responsible for designing an innovative, learner-centric approach to education techniques. Her 2013 book “Source-full Intelligence: Understanding uniqueness and Oneness through Education” was one of the 18 best published books in the USA.

Visual Artist

Mehak Malhotra

Mehak Malhotra

Presenting to you, the next speaker for TEDxSomaiyaVidyavihar 2019, Mehak Malhotra. Mehak is a graduate of NIFT where she won the best graduation award. Currently, she is the CEO of Giggling Monkey Studio where she has successfully designed many cool and popular logos that we see daily. She has won many accolades like the 25 under 25 by campus diaries, VH1 and TATA Nano in 2016. Her work has been featured in several prestigious fashion magazines like Rolling Stones and Vogue. She also speaks at various colleges in order to influence people to pursue alternate careers. She has also spoken about issues that she believes need a Paradigm Shift through her much acclaimed matchbox series. Mehak has worked closely with musicians to produce music videos that are nationally and internationally acclaimed.

Nuru Karim

Nuru Karim

Meet Nuru Karim, Founder and Design Principal of Nudes, a practice which operates within the realms of cross-disciplinary cultures of public art, architecture-urbanism and computational design optimisation / fabrication powered by digital 'making' tools that address large networks of social, cultural and environmental problems by solving them. He received his Masters in Architecture and Urbanism from the Architectural Association [AADRL] London, United Kingdom. He has worked for Zaha Hadid Architects, London. His undergraduate studies included travel and education in the cities of Mumbai, Montreal, New York and London. Nuru's projects have received critical recognition both for their built work in competition and also design. His works have received several awards including a nomination for the Aga Khan Award for Architecture in the 2013 cycle.

Culture Editor at The Swaddle

Nadia Nooreyezdan

Nadia Nooreyezdan

Nadia Nooreyezdan is the culture editor of The Swaddle, which is a parenting and family health site for a new generation of Indian parents. Her articles cover issues at the intersection of gender and politics. She is invested in feminist theory, pop culture, and queering everything.
