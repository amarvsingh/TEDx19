Founder & Director - Earth5R

Saurabh
Gupta

Saurabh Gupta

A renowned environmentalist and a social entrepreneur, this young revolutionary left his lucrative job at Cafe Coffee Day to bicycle around different countries for 50,000 kilometres in search for a purpose. Meet Saurabh Gupta, the founder and director of Earth5R, a Mumbai based global environmental organization. Winner of the Young Leader India-France award by the President of France, Emmanuel Macron, and the project leader of Paris Peace Forum, this exciting man is bringing a Paradigm Shift, one locality at a time.
